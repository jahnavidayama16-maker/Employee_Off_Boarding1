Employee Off-boarding Manual

A step-by-step interactive guide to navigating the corporate separation process.

Log into the Employee Portal and navigate to your active dashboard. Click on Raise Resignation to fill out the standard termination form. You are required to upload a formal PDF resignation letter and define your expected final working day.

Your request is automatically queued for your direct Manager. You can track their decision in real-time on your dashboard using the Active Resignation Tracker. If rejected, the manager is required to provide a mandatory corporate justification.

Upon successful Manager approval, a classified Exit Interview form unlocks on your dashboard. You must submit this critical feedback before your request can legally proceed to the HR Clearance layer.

HR automatically triggers cross-department checklists (IT, Finance, Library) and collects your hardware assets. Once the Admin signs off on the final exit, the system will execute a Soft-Delete Protocol, permanently deactivating your internal authentication access.
